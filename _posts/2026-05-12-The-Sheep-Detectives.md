**Review:**
I don’t have a ton of thoughts about this movie, but I thought it was simple and fun. The overarching story was too simple and cliche for me to fall in love with, especially since the murder mystery was too obvious to solve. Even though the movie did focus on trying to determine who the killer was, it also did a great job at weaving in some very wholesome messages throughout. It’s not a bad movie by any means, and I really can see why so many adults love this movie, but I found the intended audience of the movie to be those who are much younger than me, being a little too cheesy. If you saw the trailers and are interested in watching this, I’d definitely go out of your way to watch it as you will probably enjoy it even more than I did.

**Rating:**
3/5
