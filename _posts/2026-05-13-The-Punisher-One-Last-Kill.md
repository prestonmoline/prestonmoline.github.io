**Review:**
I love Jon Bernthal as The Punisher, and I really love what he was able to do with his character across his TV show and across the Daredevil show. This Special Presentation gives you even more of the Frank Castle that you love, having the character moments and action you will want. My main struggle with this Special Presentation is that it was just too short. 45 minutes is not enough time to set-up a story and finish it in a complete package, especially for a character like Frank Castle. You need to see him starting to involve himself in something, and then you need to see it implode on him, having him struggle to get his way out. This mostly happened during the presentation, but it was a simplified, condensed version of what it should have been. 

**Rating:**
3.5/5
