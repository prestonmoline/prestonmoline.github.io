**Short Review:**
This movie is simply fantastic. Ryland Grace (Ryan Gosling) and Rocky are a dynamic duo that makes every scene they are in together electric. The score is incredible, and in combination with the beautiful visuals, this movie was a joy to watch. Despite being a movie focused on space, it has a lot of heart, spending as much time with the people in the story to make you connect with and fall in love with them, especially Ryland and Rocky.

I could keep describing why it is so good, but this is one you NEED to watch for yourself. Watch it for yourself, especially if you have read the book.

**Rating:**
4.5/5
